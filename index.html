About Me

Hi. My name is Sam Lacey and I love dogs! I’ve always loved animals and had a particular soft spot for dogs having grown up in a house full of Irish Wolfhounds and Great Danes. Unfortunately for me my husband, Tom, was the total opposite having grown up with Patterdale Terriers so when we moved in together we decided to get something a little more… well, little. That’s where we came into Belle, our gorgeous Dalmation. Together we share a house in Haslingden and when I’m not walking dogs all day we love to go camping across the UK in our custom trailer.

Being a dog walker was always one of those dreams I had which I thought would never come true, but back in 2019 I had a job that I really hated; I came home every day miserable and I had just had enough. So after a few conversations with Tom and a few sleepless nights, I handed in my notice to start up Plods ‘n’ Paws. I was scared that it was a terrible idea and I would come to regret it, but after months of grafting and word of mouth from the customers I had managed to get, it really took off. That brings us to where we are today. During the lockdown I wanted to expand my horizons so I took courses on training and grooming so that I could offer nearly everything you would need as a pet owner.

I can't wait to meet you and your furry friends!

Sam
